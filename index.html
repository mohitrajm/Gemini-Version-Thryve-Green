Benefits for Businesses and the Environment

Biomass briquettes offer a cleaner alternative to coal and firewood. They help reduce emissions, support sustainability goals, and provide a reliable source of energy for industrial use.

A Practical and Cleaner Fuel Choice

Industries in India are looking for cleaner energy options that do not disrupt operations. Biomass briquettes make this shift possible. They burn cleaner than coal and are more consistent than firewood, which makes them suitable for many industrial heating applications.

Key Benefits for Industries

Cleaner fuel compared to coal

Briquettes release less sulfur, smoke, and ash while maintaining stable heat output.

Better for ESG goals

Using renewable fuel supports your company’s sustainability reporting and environmental commitments.

Consistent form

Uniform briquettes are easier to store, handle, and feed into boilers compared to loose firewood.

Stable supply

We focus on consistent quality and reliability so you can run your operations smoothly.

Responsible sourcing

Your fuel choice helps reduce stubble burning and supports farmers, adding social value to your business.

About Thryve Green

We want to turn India’s waste into clean energy, useful materials, and better solutions that reduce pollution and support people.

Our Origin

Why We Started Thryve Green

We started Thryve Green with a simple goal. Build something clean and practical that helps India reduce pollution without placing extra burden on people. Our first idea was to create products using bamboo. While researching that path, we realized how much agricultural waste India produces every year and how most of it ends up burned in the open.

At the same time, industries continue to rely heavily on coal and firewood. Both problems meet at the same point.

Too much waste.
High demand for energy.
And very few long-term solutions that connect the two.

That is where we found opportunity. Even though biomass briquettes is a mature sector, India’s waste challenge is still massive. There is space for responsible companies to collect and convert this material instead of letting it burn. Starting here made sense. It is practical, proven, and builds the right foundation for our long-term vision.

Our Belief and Commitment

We believe sustainability should be simple. When solutions are simple, people use them. When they work on the ground, they last. Our focus is to create products that:

  • Reduce pollution
  • Support farmers
  • Help industries shift to cleaner fuel
  • Make waste useful
  • Prepare India for higher energy demand

We are not building a one-product company. We are building a platform to turn waste into value.

Where We Are Going

Briquettes are our starting point. Over time, we want to explore more waste-based products that can replace fossil materials. This includes options like biomass-based fuels, biochar, bio-bitumen, and other clean alternatives that support industry and reduce environmental impact.

Our mission is clear. Turn India’s waste into clean energy and useful materials in a way that is practical for both businesses and communities.
